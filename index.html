Get Ready with Reggie

ReggieReady is a lightweight, standalone assistant for facilities teams. It synthesizes public documentation, best practices, and user-provided context to produce concise, step-by-step guidance. It is not connected to any private systems; we do not use institutional data without explicit permission.
